    I go through phases where I memorialize each rock show I attend with a haiku. Here are a few of my favorites:
    Jayhawks
    So Midwest, serving
    Our collective elegy
    Like it's apple pie
    Seattle Rock Lottery
    Disarrayed bands dodge
    Conflicting gravities, like
    Tight roping trip wires
    Low
    That's how to hold back,
    Wear fervor like a bridle,
    A necklace of birds.
    The Ponys
    Three chords make rock songs.
    Sometimes, that's par for the course.
    Other times? Loud golf.
